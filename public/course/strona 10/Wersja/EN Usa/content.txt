Let's start with phone settings. If your smartphone allows it, turn on:

**Portrait Mode**
What does it do?
The phone will blur the background, making the dish the main focus.

**RAW Mode**
What does it do?
The photo will be better quality and easier to correct later without losing quality during editing.
